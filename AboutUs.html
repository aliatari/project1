About us



Why?

Movie-of-All-Time gathers all the hostest and most popular movies of all time. Weither you are searching for new movies that just came out or old movies since 1920's, you will find them all on Movie-of-All-Time. You don't have to dig through google or multiple movie websites to look for you favorite movie anymore! Movie-of-All-Time is here to help you reduce your headache, save time on your searching process, and enjoying your time on your favorite movies.

As film lovers ourselves, we became frustrated by how difficult it was to do a simple search, so we decided to gather together, puting out our best work and shut out the best movie website of all time!

What is Movie-of-All-Time?

It's all about making your search easier on all time movies, by movie genres or movie names, and where the movies are showing.

Movie-of-All-Time is a new and innovative type of search engine for movie listings, you can find all information about the movie, see movie trailers, posters, synopses, reviews, cast members and loads more..You can also find the location where the movies are showing or where you can watch your movies if the movies are no longer available in theathers.

On Movie-of-All-Time, we use a cool movie database which helps you find information about a movie quickly.

Movie-of-All-Time is currently available in the United States and with more countries coming soon. The Movie-of-All-Time team is located in Seattle WA, US.

It’s still in early stage for us at Movie-of-All-Time, but we plan to add more new and cool features which help on simplifying the user experience in the coming months.
